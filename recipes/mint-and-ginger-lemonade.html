Mint Ginger Lemonade

Original recipe: A beautiful Plate

Description

Fresh homemade lemonade infused with homemade mint ginger simple syrup, and served over mint ice cubes. A refreshing drink for spring or summer!

  • Prep Time: 45 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 55 mins
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

Mint Ginger Simple Syrup:

  • ¾ cup (150 g) granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup (180 mL) water
  • ½ cup packed mint leaves torn
  • 1-inch piece fresh ginger diced

Lemonade:

  • 3 cups (720 mL) cold water
  • ¾ cup (180 mL) freshly squeezed lemon juice. Roughly 6 to 7 large lemons
  • mint ginger syrup
  • fresh mint leaves
  • 1 lemon sliced thinly

Steps

    Prepare Simple Syrup:

  1. Combine all of the simple syrup ingredients in a small saucepan. Bring to a low simmer, and stir until the sugar has dissolved completely
  2. Boil and simmer the syrup for an additional 1 to 2 minutes. Remove and place in a heatproof bowl
  3. Allow to come to room temperature, or alternatively cover and infuse in the fridge for at least 30 to 45 minutes or overnight (this will yield a more intensely flavored lemonade)
  4. Once chilled, strain simple syrup through a fine-meshed sieve, discarding the mint leaves and ginger pieces

    5. Make Lemonade:

  5. In a large pitcher, combine the cold water, lemon juice, and a full batch of room temperature or chilled mint ginger simple syrup. Stir well
  6. Add fresh mint and sliced lemon slices to the pitcher as desired
  7. Serve lemonade chilled or alternatively, over homemade mint ice cubes (simply add fresh mint leaves to your ice cube tray, along with water, and freeze)
